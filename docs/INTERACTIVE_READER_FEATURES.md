# Interactive Reader Features Guide

## Quick Start

The Interactive Linear Reader transforms reading into an engaging, customizable experience. Here's what you can do:

## üéØ Key Features

### 1. Real-Time Reading Stats
**Location**: Top-right floating toolbar

Shows you:
- **Progress**: How far through the story (0-100%)
- **Time**: Estimated reading time remaining
- **Words**: Total word count

Updates automatically as you scroll.

### 2. Customizable Typography
**Access**: Click the ‚öôÔ∏è settings icon (top-right)

Adjust:
- **Font Size**: 14px to 24px (great for accessibility)
- **Line Spacing**: 1.4x to 2.2x (find your comfort zone)

**Keyboard Shortcuts**:
- `Ctrl + [` : Make text smaller
- `Ctrl + ]` : Make text larger

### 3. Smart Progress Tracking

**Left Spine**:
- Amber progress bar fills as you read
- Circular badge shows exact percentage
- Glowing effect for visual appeal

**Bottom of Text**:
- "Paragraph X of Y"
- Percentage complete
- Helps you pace your reading

### 4. Paragraph Focus
As you scroll, the current paragraph **brightens** while others **dim slightly**. This helps you:
- Stay focused on what you're reading
- Reduce eye strain
- Track your position easily

### 5. Enhanced Quote Saving

**To Save a Quote**:
1. Highlight any text
2. Click "Save Quote" button that appears
3. Quote instantly appears in right panel

**Quote Panel Features**:
- Numbered quotes (newest first)
- Timestamps for each save
- Hover to reveal "Remove" button
- "Copy All Quotes" button at bottom

**Toggle Panel**: Click üí¨ icon or press `Ctrl + \`

### 6. Quote Management

**Individual Quotes**:
- Hover over any quote card
- Click "Remove" to delete
- Long quotes auto-truncate (shows first 150 chars)

**Export All**:
- Click "Copy All Quotes" button
- Pastes formatted list to clipboard
- Perfect for notes or sharing

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Adjust font size** before starting - find your sweet spot
2. **Use paragraph focus** to maintain concentration
3. **Save quotes as you read** - don't wait until the end
4. **Copy quotes** before leaving - they're session-based for now
5. **Toggle quotes panel** if you want distraction-free reading
6. **Watch the progress bar** for motivation on long stories
