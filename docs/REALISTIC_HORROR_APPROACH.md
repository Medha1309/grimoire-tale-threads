## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

### Core Principles:

1. **Subtlety Over Shock**
   - Hint at horror, don't show it all
   - Let imagination fill gaps
   - Build atmosphere slowly

2. **Realism Over Fantasy**
   - Real creatures (spiders, flies)
   - Real physics (lamp swing, candle flicker)
   - Real textures (aged paper, scratches)
   - Real darkness (not stylized)

3. **Atmosphere Over Action**
   - Environmental storytelling
   - Mood through lighting
   - Tension through pacing
   - Dread through darkness

4. **Consistency Over Variety**
   - Unified dark aesthetic
   - Consistent horror tone
   - No tonal shifts
   - Sustained experience
