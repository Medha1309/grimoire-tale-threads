üéØ Quick Test Workflow

  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Navigate to /art-studio
  3. Select brush tool (or press B)
  4. Choose a color from the color picker
  5. Draw on the canvas
  6. Add a new layer (Ctrl+N)
  7. Draw on the new layer
  8. Toggle layer visibility
  9. Test undo/redo (Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z)
  10. Export as PNG (Ctrl+S)
  11. Toggle Gothic Mode to see atmospheric effects
